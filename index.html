Malcolm Jones

I am a PhD student in mathematics at Te Herenga Waka--Victoria University of Wellington, New Zealand, supervised by Lisa Orloff Clark and Astrid an Huef.

My main research interests are in studying connections between inverse semigroups, groupoids, higher-rank graphs and their operator algebras.

Since 2022 I have been a member of the New Zealand Mathematical Society.
