Malcolm Jones

I am a PhD student in mathematics at Victoria University of Wellington. My supervisors are Lisa Orloff Clark and Astrid an Huef.

My main research interests are in studying connections between inverse semigroups, groupoids, higher-rank graphs and their operator algebras. My other interests include plants, coffee and music.

Since 2022 I have been a member of the New Zealand Mathematical Society and the Australia and New Zealand Industrial and Applied Mathematics (ANZIAM) division of the Australian Mathematical Society. Also, I am on the organising committee of the New Zealand Mathematics and Statistics Postgraduate Conference to be held in 2023.
